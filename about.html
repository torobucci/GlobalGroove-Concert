Global groove concert 2023

Global Groove Concerts provide a way for musicians and performers to connect with their audience and share their music and art. We provide oportunity for artists to showcase their talents and for fans to enjoy their favorite music live. This year we hope to expand our invitations to organization and well know individuals.

If you would like to know more about Global Groove, please direct your message to the following email address: globalgroove2023@kenya.co.ke Our team will be happy to assist you with any questions or concerns you may have.
